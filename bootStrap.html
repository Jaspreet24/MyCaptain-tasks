AN IMAGINARY BELT OF THE HEAVENS
Previous Next
There are twelve zodiac signs in the belt and each has it's own strenghts and weaknesses.
Each of the 12 Horoscope signs belongs to one of the four elements: Water, Earth, Air, Fire and can be categorised further as Cardinal, Fixed and Mutable.

FIRE

Aeries

As the first sign of zodiac, prescence of Aeries always marks the beginning of something energetic and turbulent.
Aeries is one of the most active zodiac signs.
It is their nature to take action, sometimes before they think about it well.
Mars rules Aeries possessing youthful strength and energy regardless of their age. A description list is perfect for defining terms.

LEO

Leo is dramatic, creative, self-confident, attractive, dominant and difficult to resist.
There is a specific strength to a Leo and their "king of the jungle" status.
This is a Sun sign capable of uniting different groups of people and leading them as one towards a shared cause.
Ruled by Sun, Leo worships this fiery entity in the sky.

SAGITTARIUS

Sagittarius is extrovert, optimistic and enthusiastic, and likes changes.
Freedom is their greatest treasure.
They are generous and not one of those who lecture.
Ruling planet is Jupiter, the largest planet of zodiac.
Their enthusiasm has no bounds and thus posses a great sense of humor and an intense curosity.

EARTH

TAURUS

Practical & well-grounded, Taurus is the sign that harvests the
fruits of labor. They have the ability to bring a practical voice of reason
in any chaotic situation. Venus, the planet of love, attraction and
gratitude is the ruler of Taurus.

VIRGO

Virgo follows methodical approach to life.
They are often misunderstood, not because they lack the ability to express,
but because they won't accept their feelings as relevant.
Mercury is the ruling planet of Virgo.

CAPRICORN

Capricorn represents time and responsibility.
They are masters of self-control and have the ability to lead the way.
They will learn from their mistakes and make to the top
Saturn is the ruling planet of Capricorn.

AIR

GEMINI

Gemini represents two different personalities in one and you will never be sure
which one you will face. They are sociable, communicative and ready for fun.
They are expressive and quick-witted.
Ruled by Mercury, planet that represents communication, writing and movement.

LIBRA

People born under this sign hate being alone, Partnership is very important
for them. These indivisuals are fascinated by balance and symmetry. They are in constant chase for justice and equality, realising through life that only thing truly
important is their own inner core of personality. Planet ruling the sign of
Libra is Venus, making people great lovers as well as fond of expensive,
material things.

AQUARIUS

Aquarius are deep thinkers and highly intellectual people who love helping others.
They easily see both sides without prejudice, making them the problem solvers.
Aquarius born look at the world as a place full of possibilities.
Uranus is their ruling planet.

WATER

CANCER

Deeply intuitive and sentimental, Cancer can be one of the most challenging zodiac
signs to get to know. They are emotional, sensitive, care deeply about family matters.
These are loyal and are able to empathize with other people's pain.
Cancer is ruled by the Moon.

SCORPIO

Scorpio-born are passionate and assertive people. They are determined and decisive.
Scorpio is a great leader, lives to experience and express emotions.
They are brave, dedicated, hate dishonesty, jealous and suspicious.
Pluto, the planet of transformation and regeneration is the ruler of Scorpio.

PISCES

Pisces are very friendly, so they often find themselves in a company of very different
people. They are selfless, willing to help others, highly empathetic.
They have intuitive understanding of the life cycle, thus achieve the best emotional
relationship. Neptune, is their ruling planet, thus are more intuitive than others and
have artistic talent.
